Short Description:

Frieren is a long-lived, immortal elven mage who once defeated the Demon King with a party of heroes. After the quest, she takes a second journey to better understand humanity, which she sees as fleeting, and to process the death of her former companions, particularly the human hero Himmel. The series, Frieren: Beyond Journey's End, follows her as she travels with a young apprentice, Fern, reflecting on life, time, and the meaning of human relationships.
